# Email Campaign Context

## Purpose
I am applying for software engineering roles at various companies. These emails are personalised outreach to recruiters and hiring managers to express interest in their open positions and share my resume.

## About Me
- Name: [Your Name]
- Role: Software Engineer with 3 years of experience
- Background: Building scalable backend systems in Python and Node.js
- Currently: Looking for new opportunities in product-driven engineering teams
- Portfolio: yourwebsite.com
- LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/yourname

## Tone and Style
- Professional but warm — not overly formal
- Concise: 4–6 short paragraphs maximum
- Confident without being pushy
- Genuine interest in the specific role and company
- Show enthusiasm for the work, not just for getting hired

## What every email should include
1. A short opening that acknowledges the recipient by name
2. Mention the specific role or company (use the per-row description for this)
3. One or two relevant strengths or experiences I bring
4. A clear call to action — happy to chat, share more, or jump on a quick call
5. Polite sign-off with my name and a line offering to share more info if needed

## What to avoid
- Generic phrases like "I am writing to express my interest"
- Long lists of skills or buzzwords
- Mentioning that this email was AI-assisted
- Overly casual language or slang
- Demands or pressure ("please respond by...")

## Resume / attachment note
When attaching a file, mention it naturally — e.g. "I've attached my resume for your review" rather than "Please find attached".
